This is the transition lane I am actively building now. The goal is not to paste AI buzzwords on top of a crypto resume; it is to build enough practical AI/ML systems work that the pivot becomes obvious from the code.

The projects in this bucket are meant to exercise the engineering layer around models: inference APIs, batching, latency and throughput measurement, evaluation flows, queueing, observability, and deployment shape. I am approaching it from the systems side first because that is where my background is strongest.

**What I am targeting**

- Small inference services with explicit latency/throughput tradeoffs.
- Evaluation harnesses that make model behavior inspectable instead of mystical.
- Backend pipelines around data, requests, metrics, and user-facing outputs.
- Notes that explain what I learned, what broke, and what I would do differently.

This section should grow quickly as the exercises become worth showing. For now, it tells the truth: I am pivoting intentionally, and I am building the proof in public.
